BLACKLIGHTDOMS

THE ORIGIN OF BLDS


before BLDS had a name,
before the symbol existed,
before the squad was formed…

there were only individuals trying to survive their own darkness.

Some were ignored.
Some were underestimated.
Some failed over and over again until they stopped expecting anyone to believe in them.

They watched others rise faster.
Watched others gain recognition.
Watched people quit the moment things became difficult.

And slowly, they realized something:

Most people only shine when the world is bright.
But very few can continue moving forward when everything turns dark.

That realization became the beginning of BLDS.

Not a squad created for fame.
Not a kingdom built on arrogance.
Not a place for people searching only for victory.
But a dominion built by those who refused to disappear.

THE BLACKLIGHT CORE


A light that never disappears in darkness.
It represents inner purpose and survival.


TEN FLAMES OF BLDS


Greater Flames

I. Vision
The ability to see direction even when everything looks lost.

II. Discipline
The strength to keep moving even when no one is watching.

III. Loyalty
To stand together through victory and defeat without leaving anyone behind.

IV. Ambition
The desire to grow beyond limits and evolve endlessly.

V. Resilience
The power to rise again after every fall, no matter how many times it happens.

Lesser Flames

I. Hope
The light that remains during the darkest moments of life.

II. Awareness
The ability to understand reality and not be blinded by illusion.

III. Patience
The endurance to grow slowly without giving up early.

IV. Courage
The will to move forward even when fear is present.

V. Unity
The bond that turns individuals into something stronger than themselves.

Though smaller, these flames are just as important.
Because even the brightest Core will fade without what supports it.

FORGOTTEN ACCESS

THE FORGOTTEN ECLIPSE


Long before BLDS became known…
before the symbol,
before the Core,
before the name itself existed…

there was another generation.
A generation erased from the archive.

They were the first ones who tried to build a dominion inside the darkness.
Not for power.
Not for glory.
But to create a place where broken people could still stand together.

At first… they succeeded.
The eclipse began to grow.

More people arrived.
The light inside the Core became stronger.

But slowly, something changed.
Some members stopped believing in the original purpose.
They became obsessed with recognition.
With status.
With becoming feared instead of becoming united.

The dominion began tearing itself apart from within.

And then came the Collapse.
No one speaks about what truly happened that night.
The old records were destroyed.
Names disappeared.
Symbols vanished.
Entire conversations were erased as if they never existed.

Only one thing remained:
A single fragment of the Core.

Hidden by the last survivor.

Legend says the survivor left behind a message:

“If the eclipse ever returns…
do not let the darkness consume the Core again.”

Years later…
BLDS was born from the remains of that forgotten eclipse.

Not as a continuation.
But as a second chance.

That is why BLDS fears one thing more than failure:
Forgetting its true purpose.

Because the greatest threat was never the outside world.
It was the darkness growing inside the dominion itself.

And somewhere…
buried beneath forgotten archives and erased memories…
the remains of the first eclipse still exist.

Waiting.
