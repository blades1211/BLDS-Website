THE ORIGIN OF BLDS


before BLDS had a name,
before the symbol existed,
before the squad was formed…

there were only individuals trying to survive their own darkness.

Some were ignored.
Some were underestimated.
Some failed over and over again until they stopped expecting anyone to believe in them.

They watched others rise faster.
Watched others gain recognition.
Watched people quit the moment things became difficult.

And slowly, they realized something:

Most people only shine when the world is bright.
But very few can continue moving forward when everything turns dark.

That realization became the beginning of BLDS.

Not a squad created for fame.
Not a kingdom built on arrogance.
Not a place for people searching only for victory.
But a dominion built by those who refused to disappear.

THE BLACKLIGHT CORE


At the center of BLDS lies the Core.
A single light that continues to exist even in absolute darkness.

The Core represents the inner purpose of every member of BLDS.

Not given by the world.
Not created by attention.
But forged through pain, failure, and survival.

It is the reminder that even when everything collapses…
even when no one is watching…
your inner light still matters.

BLDS believes the strongest are not those who never fall,
but those who continue rising after every fall.

MEANING


The Eclipse → Darkness, pressure, failure, the world itself
The Core → Inner purpose, identity, and will to survive

It is not about avoiding darkness.
It is about not letting darkness consume you.

TEN FLAMES OF BLDS


Greater Flames
I. Vision — Seeing direction even in chaos
II. Discipline — Staying consistent when no one watches
III. Loyalty — Standing together through victory and defeat
IV. Ambition — The desire to surpass limits and continue evolving
V. Resilience — The strength to rise again after every fall

Lesser Flames
I. Hope — The light that remains during difficult times
II. Awareness — The ability to stay conscious and understand reality
III. Patience — Growth takes time and endurance
IV. Courage — The will to move forward despite fear
V. Unity — The bond that turns individuals into something greater

Though smaller, these flames are just as important.
Because even the brightest Core will fade without what supports it.

FORGOTTEN ACCESS


THE FORGOTTEN ECLIPSE


Long before BLDS existed…
there was a generation erased from the archive.

They built the first dominion inside darkness.
But it collapsed from within.

Names were erased. Records lost.

Only one message remained:
“If the eclipse returns… don’t repeat the past.”

BLDS is not continuation.
It is a second chance.
