THE ORIGIN OF BLDS

Before BLDS had a name...

Before the symbol existed.
Before the squad was formed.
Before the eclipse began...

There were only individuals trying to survive their own darkness.

Some were ignored.
Some were underestimated.
Some failed over and over again until they stopped expecting anyone to believe in them.

They watched others rise faster.
Watched others gain recognition.
Watched people quit the moment things became difficult.

And slowly, they realized something:

Most people only shine when the world is bright.
But very few can continue moving forward when everything turns dark.

That realization became the beginning of BLDS.

Not a squad created for fame.
Not a kingdom built on arrogance.
Not a place for people searching only for victory.

But a dominion built by those who refused to disappear.

A place where failure was never the end.
A place where loyalty mattered more than status.
A place where darkness was not feared... but embraced.

BLDS was never meant to follow the light.

It was created to become the eclipse itself.



THE CORE

At the center of the eclipse lies the Core.

A single star that continues to shine no matter how dark the sky becomes.

The Core represents the inner purpose carried by every member of BLDS.

Not a purpose given by the world.
Not a purpose created by attention or popularity.
But a purpose discovered through hardship.

It is the reminder that:

Even when everything around you changes…
Even when people leave…
Even when failure surrounds you…
Your inner light can still remain alive.

BLDS believes the strongest people are not those who never fall.

The strongest are the ones who continue standing after every fall.

That is why the Core never disappears.

Because purpose cannot be erased by fear.
